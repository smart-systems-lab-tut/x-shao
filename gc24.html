Bio

  • I am currently working as an Associate Professor with tenure at The University of Electro-Communications (UEC), Japan.

  • I graduated from National Institute of Informatics and University of Science and Technology of China with a Ph.D. and a B.S., respectively.

  • I have been serving or have seved as Editor for IEEE Network, IEEE Transactions on Multimedia, IEEE Internet of Things Journal, IEEE Open Journal of the Computer Society, and Wireless Networks. I have been serving or have seved as guest editor for IEEE Wireless Communications and IEEE Vehicular Technology Magazine

  • I have been serving as area chair of ACM MM24, area chair of IEEE ICME23, symbosium chair of IEEE Globecom23, TPC co-chair of IEEE Infocom23 NG-OPERA workshop, General co-chair of IEEE ICC23 DDINS workshop, Workshop co-chair of IEEE HPSR22, TPC co-chair of IEEE ICC22 DDINS workshop, Track co-chair of IEEE VTC 2021, TPC co-chair of ICT-DM2021, Poster and demo session co-chair of IEEE MSN2020, etc.

  • I currently serve as the Web Chair of IEEE Technical Committee on Big Data and Liaison Officer of IEEE Technical Committee on Hyper-intelligence

  • I have published more than 30 papers in ICC and Globecom, and have received best paper awards from IEEE MSN 2020, IEEE ICOIN 2008, IEEE CBDCOM2018, IEEE ICPADS2021 (outstanding paper runner-up) etc. I am the receipt of the IPSJ/IEEE Computer Society Young Computer Researcher Award, IEEE ComSoc MMTC outstanding young researcher award and IEICE young researcher award
